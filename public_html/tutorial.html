Tutorial

  • Show a list of survey's targets: /nps-list-targets
    Note all members are targets by default

  • Remove member(s) from the target list: /nps-remove-targets
    Examples
    • /nps-remove-targets @peter @amy

  • Add member(s) to the target list: /nps-add-targets
    Examples
    • /nps-add-targets @peter @amy

  • Schedule a survey: /nps-schedule-survey
    Note only one schedued or active survey is allowed at any time

    Examples
    • once now: /nps-schedule-survey --now
    • periodically:
      • weekly: /nps-schedule-survey --weekly
      • monthly: /nps-schedule-survey --monthly
      • quarterly: /nps-schedule-survey --quarterly

  • Remind silent members to complete the survey: /nps-send-reminder

  • Unschedule or close a survey: /nps-stop-survey

  • Get the summary of closed surveys: /nps-summary
    • Examples
    • view all: /nps-summary --all
    • view the latest: /nps-summary 1
    • view last 10: /nps-summary 10

  • Retrieve the result of closed surveys: /nps-export-result
    • Examples
    • view all: /nps-export-result --all
    • view the latest: /nps-export-result 1
    • view last 10: /nps-export-result 10

  • Know what's happening now: /nps-status

  • Seek help or reference to use the app: /nps-help
